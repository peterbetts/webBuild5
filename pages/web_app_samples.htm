Featured Web App : Calculatorn't
A calculator that occasionally provides incorrect answers. I maintain the position that if you can't do the math in your head, it's not worth doing. This calculator aims to spread that philosophy by instilling a nagging doubt in the user as to whether they'll get the correct answer. I plan to pitch the app to the Nova Scotia Department of Education and Early Childhood Development as a training aid for early schooling. Gotta start 'em young, you know?

Tech stack:
HTML, CSS, JavaScript
